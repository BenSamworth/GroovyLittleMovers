Katy

Hi, I'm Katy, Mum of 4 and Creator and Director of Groovy Little Movers. My passion for Dance started at school. So after taking my A levels at college, I went to London to train at The Laban Centre and gain a B.A (hons) degree in Dance Theatre. I worked for several years as a professional dancer, touring with companies both here and abroad, before doing a P.G.C.E in Dance and Drama and qualifying as a teacher. I enjoyed a variety of teaching roles, from Head of Dance in a Performing Arts College, to Gifted and Talented Coordinator and Dance Consultant to Primary and Secondary schools across the region. As my family grew, I moved into part time teaching, alongside Freelance contracts which fitted around my family. I started to build strong links with the DanceXchange and Birmingham Royal Ballet and over the last 15 years, I have worked for them as a Guest Artist, Choreographer, Mentor to company dancers as well as an Educational Consultant.

Being at home with my children in their early years was hugely important to me. The more children I had, the more I realised just how significant these years are to an individual's overall growth and development. As I emerged from the challenges of this stage of parenting, I was searching for a fresh way to use my skill and expertise, yet balance work and family.

With the creation of Groovy Little Movers, I have combined my love for children and my own experience as a Mum; with my skills as a Teacher and an Artist. I have created classes to support your child's learning and development in these important early years, whilst strengthening the parent/carer bond and encouraging communication and social interaction. I hope that your experience of Groovy Little Movers will be engaging and educational; as well as active and great fun.

Give your child a great start... put a big smile on your face... become a Groovy Little Mover!
