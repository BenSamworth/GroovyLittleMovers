Parties

Groovy Little Movers offers a high energy and fabulously fun party for children 1-8 years old. Parties are tailor made to your individual requirements. Simply chose the venue and we will bring the party to you. Parties cost £185.

We provide:

  • An hour long set - 15 minutes of 'welcome' time as guests arrive, followed by 45 minutes of fast paced, tailor made moving and grooving. This will be delivered by a fully trained Groovy Little Movers Teacher, who is fully insured, with a current, valid DBS certificate.
  • The full Groovy set up; including floor mats, puppets, props and instruments as chosen by you.

You provide:

  • Access to your chosen venue at least 30 minutes before the party start time. This is to allow the party leader to set up and carry out the required health and safety checks.
  • A birthday child or children and the appropriate number of guests. (see guest numbers below).

In order for parties to be successful and great fun; yet safe for all, there is a limit on the numbers of participants allowed for each age range. This must be adhered to in order for our insurance to be valid. If there is a mix of ages, please discuss this on your booking form. Please note that the maximum number of participants is always 18.

Age range Number of participants
1 - 2 years 18
3 - 5 years 16
6 - 8 years 15

Now you have the info, if you want to book, simply email us with your proposed date and venue and let's hope the calendar is clear!
